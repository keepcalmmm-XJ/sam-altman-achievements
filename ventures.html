Investment Portfolio Overview

Sam Altman has built a diverse portfolio of ventures and investments across various sectors, including artificial intelligence (AI), energy, healthcare/biotech, finance, and social media. His personal portfolio value is estimated at $2.8 billion as of December 2024.
