Sam Altman - Achievements & Awards

About Sam Altman

Sam Altman is an American entrepreneur, investor, and programmer. He is the CEO of OpenAI and was formerly the president of Y Combinator. Throughout his career, he has received numerous accolades for his contributions to technology and entrepreneurship.

Achievements & Awards

  • 2008: Recognized as one of the 'Best Young Entrepreneurs in Technology' by BusinessWeek.
  • 2015: Named the top investor under 30 by Forbes magazine.
  • 2017: Honored with the Ric Weiland Award by GLAAD for contributions to LGBTQ acceptance in the tech industry.
  • 2017: Awarded an honorary degree by the University of Waterloo.
  • April 2023: Included in the TIME100 list.
  • June 2023: Featured on the cover of Time magazine.
  • September 2023: Honored on the TIME100 AI list.
  • 2023: Named CEO of the Year by Time Magazine.
  • 2023: Received the first 'golden visa' from the government of Indonesia.
  • 2023: Received the Outstanding Alum Award from John Burroughs School.
  • 2024: Awarded the annual Experiment Cup by Xfund at Harvard University.
